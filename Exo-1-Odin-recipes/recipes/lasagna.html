Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is
so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • mozzarella
    • ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
